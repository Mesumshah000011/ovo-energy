Tango Energy vs Ovo Energy: Who Handles Billing Issues Better?

Let's be real — dealing with billing problems isn't anyone's idea of fun. A strange charge, a missing payment, or a confusing bill can turn something simple into a major headache. That's exactly why we're taking a closer look at something important: Tango Energy vs Ovo Energy — which one is better when you hit a billing snag?

If you're trying to decide between the two, or even thinking about switching providers, this breakdown will help you figure out which one fits you best.

Quick Overview of Both Providers

Before we jump into billing, let's talk a bit about who these two companies are.

Tango Energy

Tango Energy is an Australian energy company known for offering low prices and simple plans. People like it because it's no-nonsense and pretty easy to use. It's also part of Pacific Hydro, which focuses on clean energy — a nice bonus if you care about sustainability.

Ovo Energy

Ovo Energy started in the UK and brought a fresh style to Australia's energy market. It's digital-first, focuses on clean energy, and tries to keep things simple and smart. Tech lovers and environmentally minded folks seem to like it.

Billing: What Actually Matters?

When you're looking at how companies handle billing, here's what really makes a difference:

  • Bills that are clear and easy to read
  • No sneaky fees or charges
  • Simple payment options
  • Quick help when things go wrong
  • Good support when you contact them

Let's break down how Tango Energy and Ovo Energy do in each of these areas.

1. Are the Bills Clear and Easy to Understand?

Tango Energy

Tango keeps their bills simple. You'll see your energy usage, the price per kilowatt-hour, your supply charge, and the total. It's all laid out clearly with no confusing language. You can also log in to their website anytime to check your bill.

Ovo Energy

Ovo sticks to digital billing. Their bills look clean, are easy to read, and come with a monthly usage summary. They even include a carbon impact tracker, which is pretty neat if you like knowing how your energy use affects the planet.

Winner: It's a tie. Both do a good job making bills easy to read.

2. How Good Is Their Support When You Have a Billing Problem?

Tango Energy

Tango has a dedicated phone line for billing support. It's open Monday to Friday, and most people say they don't wait long. One phone call often sorts things out. You can also email them, but that might take up to two days.

Ovo Energy

Ovo is all about online support. You can reach them through email or live chat, and they've got a detailed help section on their website. They don't offer a phone number in Australia for billing, which some people might not like.

Winner: Tango Energy. Being able to call and talk to someone quickly gives them an edge.

3. What Payment Options Are Available?

Tango Energy

Tango keeps it basic — BPAY, credit card, direct debit, and Centrepay. They don't have lots of extras, but these cover most needs. If you need more time to pay, you can ask for an extension or hardship help, but you'll need to call them.

Ovo Energy

Ovo prefers monthly direct debit. They also offer a digital tool called an Energy Plan Manager, which helps balance payments with usage. If you're facing financial trouble, they do offer flexible payment solutions.

Winner: Ovo Energy. Their billing tools make it easier to stay on top of things.

4. Do They Send Helpful Reminders?

Tango Energy

Tango sends you basic reminders before the bill is due. Depending on your settings, you'll get an email or a text. Miss a payment? They'll follow up with another reminder a few days later.

Ovo Energy

Ovo takes it a step further. They send alerts, tips, and usage warnings. Their mobile-first system gives you updates that actually help you manage energy use better.

Winner: Ovo Energy. Their reminders feel smarter and more helpful.

What Happens If There's a Mistake on Your Bill?

Tango Energy

If there's an error on your bill, just give Tango a call or shoot them an email. They say most issues get sorted in 2 to 5 business days. Their team is trained to handle these fast.

Ovo Energy

You'll need to fill out an online form if something's wrong with your Ovo bill. The process is clear, and they keep you in the loop, but the fix might take a bit longer without phone support.

Winner: Tango Energy. Talking to someone right away helps get things fixed faster.

Verdict: Who Does Billing Better?

Here's the simple breakdown:

  • Choose Tango Energy if you want quick phone support and simple billing with no surprises.
  • Pick Ovo Energy if you prefer smart tools, digital notifications, and don't mind skipping the phone calls.

Both are good at handling billing, but Tango Energy comes out slightly ahead if you value quick help and traditional support.

Final Thoughts

Let's be honest — dealing with billing should be easy. And with both Ovo Energy and Tango Energy, you're in decent hands. They just have different ways of doing things.

Want something simple with a real person to talk to? Go for Tango.

More into apps and digital tools? Ovo could be your match.

Either way, now you've got the info to choose what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Does Ovo Energy offer phone support in Australia?

No, they don't have a phone number for billing support. They stick to email and live chat.

Q: Can I change my payment method with Tango Energy?

Yes, you can. But you might have to call them to update it.

Q: Which one is better for people who like digital tools?

That would be Ovo Energy. Their tools and smart alerts are built for online users.

Q: What if I miss a payment?

Both providers send reminders. Tango follows up with a text or email. Ovo sends smart alerts to help you catch up.

Q: Is it hard to dispute a charge?

Tango makes it easier with phone support. Ovo handles disputes online, which may take a bit longer.
